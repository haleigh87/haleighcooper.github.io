The night was set for success with not one but THREE women hosting the oscars, two of which were women of color. Sunday night was set to be a big event for women of color, especially with the the first openly queer Afro-Latina, Ariana DeBose, winning an acting Oscar, or more specifically Best Supporting Actress. Various other important moments happened, from only the second deaf award winning actor, Troy Kotsur,winning best supporting actor, to Best Actress Jessica Chastain speaking on issues such as suicide and anti-LGBTQ legislation.

But alas, the only thing anyone can seem to talk about is Will Smith. Not about the first Oscar he has ever won, nor that it is Best Actor, but the fact that he slapped Chris Rock on stage after he made an inappropriate joke about Will Smith's wife and actress Jada Pickett-Smith
